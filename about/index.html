About GVPT Program

Internationalization of the curriculum is vital to undergraduate education in Government and Politics. Upon graduation many of our students pursue employment in government, with inter-governmental or international non-governmental agencies, or go on to pursue graduate degrees in law or the social sciences. All of these paths require in depth understanding of plural societies, inter-cultural competency and context sensitive problem solving skills.

Our goals are the following:

  • Educate quality global citizens by way of International Cultural Competency.
  • Develop International Political Acumen within our students.
  • Improve International Problem Solving Skills by teaching students tools of political analysis.
